Welcome to Double Shot on Main — your perfect getaway in the heart of Kentucky's Bourbon Trail! Whether you're here for bourbon, wine, horses, or sports, you'll find plenty of in-house fun, including a fully equipped game room. Ideal for double date getaways, friend trips, or couples, this 1469 sq ft shotgun-style house offers double the fun and comfort of other rentals.

Double Shot on Main features two bedrooms, two bathrooms, and a range of amenities that make it perfect for relaxing and entertaining.

  • Game Room: Air hockey, poker/blackjack table, lounge with bar tables, dart board, glassware.
  • Kitchen: Fully equipped, coffee area with drip pot, French press, grinder, tea, and condiments.
  • Living Room: 40" Roku TV, Xbox 360, books, board games, Anderson-opoly.
  • Master Bedroom: Queen bed, en suite bathroom, 40" Roku TV, electric fireplace, private closet.
  • Guest Bedroom: Queen bed, access to game room bathroom, shared walk-in closet with essentials.
  • Deck: Seating for 4, gas grill, parking at the rear.

House Rules:

  • No Pets - to maintain a clean environment for all guests.
  • Stay and play at your own risk. Trip insurance recommended.
